Kelsey Grentzer

Student | Journalist | Photographer

My name is Kelsey Grentzer, and I'm a fourth-year journalism and sustainability student at the University of Florida with experience in reporting, photography, videography, copy editing and marketing.

My writing and photography have been published in The Gainesville Sun, the Independent Florida Alligator, INsite Magazine, The Fine Print, The Suncoast News and other publications.

I have always had a meticulous sense for grammar and spelling, a natural curiosity and a creative eye for photography, and I love using these skills to learn and share others' stories. Some of my special interests include travel, nature and environmental issues.
